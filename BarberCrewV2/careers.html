Senior Barber - Application

Job responsibilities

  • Working with the team and delivering a professional, high level service to customers, whilst maintaining the facilities to a fantastic standard.
  • To lead by example and as a senior member of the staff in communication, presentation and dealing with customers.
  • Ensure that the business targets for the studio are achieved, and relevant action is taken to address any shortfall.
  • Working consistently to bring new customers to the studio through social media, leafleting, and word of mouth.

Essential Requirements

  • High energy, ambitious and professional
  • At least 3-5 years of experience in barbering and or ladies' hairdressing
  • Must have the right to work in the UK
  • Must be able to drive, or be comfortable with commuting to all three of our locations

Job Types: Full-time, Part-time Contract

Benefits

  • Bonus/Commission scheme, Company events and incentives, professional training allowance. Full time contracts and self-employed contracts available

We are an equal opportunities employer and look forward to seeing a diverse range of applicants!

Retail Assistant - Application

We are initially looking for someone part time in Twyford for weekend work to help assist the senior barbers/stylists and manage customers and the facilities. We are open to someone working Friday/Saturday/Sunday initially before going full time 5 days a week.

Job responsibilities

  • Meet and greet clients.
  • Provide refreshments and check the clients comfort.
  • You will be trained to shampoo and massage clients hair and scalp, using professional products.
  • You will be trained on all products.
  • Responsible for keeping the salon looking tidy and clean.
  • We offer full training for this position, no real experience required. An interest in hair and fashion would support your application.

Essential Requirements

  • High energy, ambitious and professional
  • At least 3 months experience in a customer service role
  • Must have the right to work in the UK
  • Must be able to drive, or be comfortable with commuting to all three of our locations

Job Types: Full-time, Part-time Contract

Benefits

  • Full time placement available at the end of the programme.
