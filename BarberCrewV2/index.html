SAFETY AT BARBER CREW

At Barber Crew, the safety of our customers and staff is at the forefront of our operations. In order to protect our community from COVID-19, we continue to follow goverment guidlines and employ the highest level of covid security:

SANITISATION - Each of our studios (Twyford, Wargrave & Wokingham) are equipped with industry standard hand sanitiser for customer and staff use.

MASKS - Masks are preferred within our studios to reduce the spread of the virus but are not required.

APPOINTMENTS - Barber Crew operates with a simple online booking system. This is to reduce the event of mass queuing and to keep our studios safe for our customers. Walk-ins are also welcome!
