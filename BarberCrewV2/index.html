SAFETY AT BARBERCREW

At Barber Crew, the safety of our customers and staff is at the forefront of our operations. In order to protect our community from COVID-19, we continue to follow goverment guidlines and employ the highest level of covid security:

SANITISATION - Each of our studios are equipped with industry standard hand sanitiser for customer and staff use.

MASKS - Masks are preferred within our studios to reduce the spread of the virus. All of our barbers wear masks during services despite government guidance not requiring it.

APPOINTMENTS - Barber Crew operates with a simple online booking system. This is to reduce the event of mass queuing and to keep our studios safe for our customers.
